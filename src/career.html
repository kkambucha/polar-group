Why work for Polar?

Polar is a highly diverse company that operates in a multicultural, global and fast-paced environment.

We are passionate about delivering the most efficient and highest quality supply chain solutions to our clients. Our goal is to build a company culture that encourages excellence, engagement, respect, education, diversity and responsibility. We constantly challenge ourselves to do more and to do better.

We value individuality highly, and encourage our employees to develop their talents in an inspiring work environment. We achieve goals together through the effective communication of thoughts and ideas, as well as by leveraging the individual skills and experiences of our people. We aim to attract and retain the best people in the logistics industry all over the world.

Our strong presence in the global logistics market offers you an excellent opportunity to develop your professional career in an international environment. Polar offers competitive incentive plans, benefits and a dynamic environment for starting and building your career.

Our global operations are divided across various regions in Europe, Russia, CIS and Asia, with a recruitment process where all applications are handled locally.
