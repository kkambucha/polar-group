Polar Plus

Traditionally, international companies operate their supply chain questions internally. As per this old status quo, large and even small companies often assume unnecessary fixed expenses to manage these operations.

Major changes and developments in the logistics industry over the last 10 years have unleashed new ideas for operating the supply chain. The new thinking is to outsource parts of or the entire supply chain to an external logistics service provider.

Polar Plus is the solution for companies seeking to outsource processes outside of their core business.

Polar Plus extends the listening, discovers and innovates beyond the general logistical thinking, and also plans, implements and operates supply chain services, among other tasks, during and after actual transportation. Our managers and HR specialists build a team of professionals to handle the desired task of extending your operations.

Polar Plus reduces costs and removes redundant steps in your supply chain.

Service include

  • Logistics outsourcing and full logistics leadership
  • Supply chain management
  • 4PL, LLP
  • Logistics administration and consulting
  • Long-term shipments and transportation management
  • Track-and-trace system that monitors the amounts due on customs duty and taxes
  • Transport and re-assembly of heavy equipment such as production plants and manufacturing facilities
  • Real-time status reports on deliveries via EDI connection to the supplier
