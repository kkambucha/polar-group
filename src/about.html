Polar's attitude

Our mission is to listen, communicate and resolve.

Our vision is to exceed your expectations as an extension of your business.

We enjoy the challenge and look forward to helping your business to grow. We don’t just take care of your logistics, we add value to your entire supply chain. To us logistics means logic in motion. To logically move forward we invest in knowledge, in people and in building an effective network, using the latest technological innovations.

Providing logistics services on an international scale makes Polar Logistics Group very aware of its ecological responsibility.

As an environmentally aware company, we are committed to protecting nature and maintaining a safe ecological environment. Our approach to reducing the environmental impact corresponds with our think local and act global business philosophy; it starts at the office desk with recycling and making more efficient use of paper and the many other resources we use every day.

When developing individually tailored logistics solutions for our customers, we also consider the ecological aspects and try to implement the most environmentally friendly procedures.

To systematically keep the environmental impact as low as possible, we strive to improve our logistics operations and processes through technical innovation. As an example, our recent ecological sustainability project included the partial and gradual conversion of our vehicle fleet to fuel-saving models that have been converted to run on liquid petroleum gas (LPG).

Our philosophy

The Polar Logistics Group provides value-added services, delivering fast, reliable and tailored supply chain solutions around the world.

In simple terms, we join the dots between our clients’ operations in Europe, the C.I.S., Russia, Asia and America, helping them to excel at what they do.

Our fully comprehensive range of services delivers efficient solutions. With a well-established business network, we can apply best practices through our team of trained, skilled professionals to offer real benefits that translate into profits for our clients across the globe.

Polar offers a wide variety of logistics services to worldwide destinations, and our clients’ requirements and goals are always our main focus. By understanding these, we can utilise our extensive knowledge and expertise in the unique aspects of the logistics industry to create optimum, bespoke solutions that will streamline their entire operation.

Growth is the true objective, and Polar finds ways to help its customers achieve it. This partnership will open up a completely new philosophy where Polar becomes an extension to the client. Tailored, intuitive and cost-efficient solutions are at our heart, and I would like to introduce our newly launched service known as Extending Logistics. This is a one-stop-shop that allows clients to create personalised ‘outside the box’ solutions and discover new and innovative services and ideas. Our mission is to listen, communicate, and resolve, and we look forward to helping your business to grow.

Esa Kataisto
Chairman
The Polar Logistics Group
