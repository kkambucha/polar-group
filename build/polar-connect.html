Polar Connect

Foreign Economic Activity (FEA) is often vital for pursuing more growth. How to tackle this project is a more complex question.

Polar Connect is your expert and partner in making the connection.

Outsourcing complete Foreign Economic Activity is our new concept for companies looking for international expansion.

Polar Connect: connecting the dots from the right product and the right supplier through to the end user. Polar Connect adds value by connecting suppliers to customers. Based on clients’ needs and objectives, we match parties and develop full FEAs and supply chains between suppliers and end users.

This dynamic approach offers both suppliers and end users a secure system to participate in Foreign Economic Activity and to enjoy the results of international trade.

Service include

  • DDP delivery, maintenance contracts and service
  • Import & Export Support for sales and pro curement departments
  • Cash flow and risk management
  • Alternative agency abroad
  • Trading partner for delivery, customs clearance and final sale
  • Trade compliance risks
  • Agents/options to sell and deliver DDU/DDP
  • Variable costs vs. fixed costs
  • Complete project support
