Polar Logistic Russia transport an oversized cargo from Germany to Russia

March 28th, 2016 - Polar Logistic Russia carried out oversized transformers form Kirkham to Novosibirsk.

In order to comply all the actual rules of transportation between Russia and European countries and to transport the transformers successfully Polar Logistics Russia realize the shipment attracting to the delivery the Belorussian carrier. The cargo was land transported and it took about 6 000 km to get the destination area in Novosibirsk in Russia. On the way transformers crossed Poland and Belorussia.

Due to the bad weather conditions in the central part of Russia the trucks slowed but achieved the address on time. Delivery “door to door” presented 15 days.

About Polar Logistics Group

Founded in 2001 in Helsinki, Finland, Polar Logistics Group is one of the leading global players in airfreight, groupage and full-load land transportation, railway transportation, multimodal transportations, customs services, door-to-door services, warehousing and supply chain management. The company also offers three innovative products, Polar Logistics, Polar Plus and Polar Connect, enabling to clients not only all the types of transportation services, but also  know-how and man-power insourcing and worldwide trading assistance.

Working in more than 115 countries, the global group Polar Logistics is an expert for automotive, engineering, iron and steel industries, chemicals sector, retail and others.
