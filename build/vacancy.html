Are you the new Polar Group custom clearance manager?

Customs Clearance Production Unit (CCPU) at Landvetter is a specialized unit for recording and performing customs clearance of all dutiable shipments within Polar Group. The department currently consists of 16 employees that the customs declare dutiable transmissions, approximately 500,000 per year.

The vacant role of Customs Clearance Manager for Polar Group Sweden is responsible for all input and perform customs clearance operations and manages and develops the work of CCPU in close collaboration with other Hubs & Gateways (H & G), the Customs Service and Polar Group functions. You will have full responsibility for costs and employees, service & quality and implementation of our strategy at local level. This is a demanding and complex role.

The service is permanent, fulltime and the starting date is immediately. Overnight stays are included in the position.

Main tasks

  • Overall responsibility for planning, directing, leading and control of the clearance process so that shortest possible lead times are achieved.
  • Maintain a safe working environment and comply with local and network regulations.
  • Responsibility for budget follow-up and control via pre-defined KPI’s for service, quality, cost effectiveness and productivity.
  • Assure the operations are conducted according to defined policies, processes and standards (ex Global SOP IMS & GAPS).
  • Lead, develop and motivate employees.
  • Develop and implement actions to support the Polar Group’ vision, strategy and goals.
  • Responsible for the Swedish Customs and other relevant authorities in regulatory compliance.
  • Continuously monitor and adapt to changes in customer needs, in law and in processes.

Qualifications/competencies:

  • Strong, direct, decisive, respectful and experienced leader with excellent social and communication skills.
  • 5-10 years leadership experience in customs clearance operations and management.
  • Experience in the transport and logistics industry.
  • Financial understanding and strong analytic skills.
  • Our corporate language is English, thus excellent language skills both written and spoken in English and Swedish are required.
  • You are a natural leader who is goal oriented with a track record of delivering your targets.
  • Driving license.

Further information

Please contact Network and Customs Manager Nicklas Blom (ph: +46709 345)
